Game Rules:

This game works like a simple board game.

Game Overview:

This game is "The Search for El Dorado." 
The year is 1540 AD. You are an explorer with a party in the New World hunting for the lost city of gold; however you are not the only ones here.
Other explorers, hostile to your party, have come looking for the ancient city.
It is a perilous journey ahead; one in which you will trek through a hazardous jungle possibly encountering hostile natives whilst you search for clues of unimaginable riches.
Will you be able to find El Dordado, the lost ancient city of gold?

Game Rules:

This game works like a simple board game.
Each square on the board is represented by a dash or a line that alternate to better distinguish spaces in between.
You press enter to roll and then your piece is moved those spaces.
There are special events for landing on certain squares on the game board indicated by a special symbol.
If landed on, the event will be indicated and its effects automatically applied.
You win the game by having more gold score than your opponent.
Gold score is gained through special events on several of the board's squares as well as by reaching the last square on the game board where El Dorado is located.
When a player lands on the last board game square, the game ends and victory goes to the player with highest gold score.
